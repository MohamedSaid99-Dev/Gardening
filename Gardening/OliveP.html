Olive

Olive, a small tree originating from the Eastern Mediterranean. It is generally cultivated in the Mediterranean Climates, most varieties are self-fertile (bredding happens in the same plant).

How to Plant:

  • Make sure to use an olive straight from the tree.
  • Remove the flesh and leave the pit to dry out for a day or two.
  • Once the pit is dry cut the pit open, be careful not to damage the seed inside.
  • You will find a small seed inside.
  • Plant the seed in a small potwith soil, an inch or two deep.
  • Place the pot in a sunny area and water daily, the soil must always be well drained.
  • An olive seed will take about 40 day to germinate.
